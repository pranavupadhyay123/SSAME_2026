Author Guidelines

  • Plagiarism Policy: The similarity index of the submitted manuscript must be less than 15% (excluding references). Checks will be performed using iThenticate/Turnitin.
  • Originality: Papers must be original and not currently under consideration for publication elsewhere.
  • Formatting: Submissions must follow the SIST, Springer format. Word and LaTeX templates are available.
