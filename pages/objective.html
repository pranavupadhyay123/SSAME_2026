Objectives & Outcomes

Objectives
  • The conference aims to serve as a dynamic hub that promotes and actively contributes to the advancement of research and developmental activities, driving the discipline towards new horizons of knowledge and application.
  • Fostering Global Collaboration and Knowledge Exchange & cultivating a vibrant ecosystem for the exchange of scientific information among a diverse community of academicians, researchers, developers, engineers, students, and practitioners worldwide.
Expected Outcomes
  • Advance the field of sustainable manufacturing by disseminating knowledge, sparking new collaborations, and identifying future research directions.
  • Empower industry professionals with practical tools and insights to implement sustainable practices and optimize their operations.
  • Bridge the gap between academia and industry by fostering dialogue and knowledge exchange between researchers and practitioners.
  • Contribute to policy development by providing evidence-based insights to policymakers and regulators.
  • Inspire future generations to pursue their career in research and higher studies.
