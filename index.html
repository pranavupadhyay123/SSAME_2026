About Conference

The conference aims to serve as an international platform for disseminating transformative research findings, enabling global academic collaboration, and fostering innovation in materials science, advanced technologies, and sustainable engineering systems. The event will convene distinguished researchers, academicians, scientists, policymakers, and industry leaders from India and abroad to discuss emerging trends that will shape the next generation of engineering materials, smart infrastructure, and sustainable manufacturing practices.

About The Institute

The North Eastern Regional Institute of Science and Technology (NERIST) is a unique Institute of its own kind in the country, having unconventional and innovative academic programmes. It is an autonomous institute under MHRD, Govt. of India. It was established in 1983 to produce technical manpower at various levels and to meet the technical requirements for the all-round development of the North Eastern States. NERIST got the status of deemed to be university on 31st May 2005 under section 3 of the UGC Act 1956. It offers modular pattern of education in all disciplines of Engineering along with Forestry...

About The Department

Department of Civil & Mechanical Engineering, NERIST, propose to organize this Two-Day International Conference. The department is committed to excellence in teaching, research, and innovation in the fields of Civil and Mechanical Engineering.
