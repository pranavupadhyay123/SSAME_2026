Authors Guidelines


Submit paper

All papers must be original and not simultaneously submitted to another journal or conference. The authors must follow the SIST format for submission of the paper.

  • Author must check plagiarism before submitting the paper. Only 10% similarity is allowed
  • Author can follow the Instruction of SIST, Springer for preparation of manuscript.
  • Click here for Latex template
  • Click here for Word template
  • Author must include their names, affiliation, address, and email ID in the manuscript
  • Camera-ready-copy (CRC): All the accepted papers should address reviewers' comments and the final version should be submitted in MS Word or Latex source including all figures. CRC must contain authors' names and affiliations. Acknowledgment and Funding sources can be included in the final CRC.
